DC 315 Certified Warnock Hersey Approved IBC Code-Compliant Thermal & Ignition Barrier Over Spray Foam Polyurethane (SPF).

The proof is in the testing settle for nothing less. View Approved Testing Matrix.

If a coating has not passed a full scale test on a manufacturer’s foam it cannot be used on that foam; there are no exceptions in the IBC Code!

DC 315 applied over Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF), is an Alternative Barrier System in “Section 2603.9 Special Approval” as a thermal barrier. To use this alternative system, a manufacturer’s SPF applied with a protective coating must be tested to the criteria of an NFPA 286, UL 1715, UL 1040, or FM 4880 for the duration of 15 minutes by an accredited fire testing facility. Products that pass an ignition barrier tested under AC 377 Appendix X are not appropriate alternative thermal barriers and cannot not be used. Depending on your particular application, either ignition or thermal barriers are required by the International Building Code (IBC). Learn More!!!

DC 315 is the most tested and approved product in the world for use as an “Alternative Barrier Coating System" on (SPF). DC 315 has passed over 100 full scale NFPA 286 tests on the majority of foam brands manufactured in the U.S.A. View Approved Testing Matrix.
