DC 315 is an interior thermal and ignition barrier coating for spray polyurethane foam insulation. Depending on your particular application, EITHER an Ignition Barrier or a Thermal Barrier is required by International Building Code (IBC). DC 315 meets the applicable requirements of 2006 IBC Section 803.2.1; 2009 IBC Section 803.2.1 and Section 2603.9; 2012 IBC Section 803.2.1 and Section 2603.10, (Special Approval for Thermal Barrier Alternatives). DC 315 also meets the requirement as an Ignition Barrier per AC 377, Appendix X at an incredible spread rate of 400 sq. ft. per gallon, therefore making DC 315 the most superior and cost effective ignition and thermal barrier on the market.    Learn More

DC 333 is a state of the art fire retardant system that uses proprietary leading edge intumescent fire retardant and fire resistance properties. DC 333 has passed countless tests from nationally certified labs and testing facilities. When exposed to high temperatures, or direct flame, DC 333 expands causing a "Char Barrier" that lifts off the surface. DC 333 offers an effective and durable barrier that cuts off the fuel source that fire needs to develop, which reduces smoke dramatically.   Learn More

DC 68 is a spray to protect flame retardant for fabric. This product can be used on natural and synthetic fibers, upholstery, furniture fabric, draperies, carpeting, decorative products, paper, burlap, canvas, wall coverings, and much more! It is odorless, colorless, non-toxic, and will not affect the look, feel, or ventilation qualities of most materials.    Learn More
